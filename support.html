Common Questions

How do I switch between sounds?
Tap any sound button to instantly switch. The app is designed for quick, effortless switching.

Does the app work in silent mode?
Yes! No BS White Noise works even when your device is in silent mode.

Why isn't there a sleep timer?
We believe in focused, minimal software. Use your device's built-in Clock app for sleep timers.

Does the app drain battery?
No BS White Noise is optimized for battery efficiency with algorithmic audio generation instead of large audio files.
